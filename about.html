About Me

I’m Lexi Mengyuan Sun, a digital media artist, and a videographer. I’m passionate about film production and 3D animation. Through videography, I could visualize my experience and ideas into video and share to others. Photographs, art gallery and the details of life and nature are my inspiration.

Currently I am a student at Simon Fraser University and major in Interactive Arts and Technology with Media Art Concentration.

If you are interested in my work, feel free to contact me at msa166@sfu.ca
